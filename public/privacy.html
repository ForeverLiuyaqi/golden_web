  2. Policy Amendments

    We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. For minor changes (e.g., typo fixes, non-material clarifications), we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this policy. For material changes (e.g., changes to data collection purposes, sharing practices, or user rights), we will notify you via in-app notification or email at least 30 days prior to the change taking effect. Your continued use of the Services following the posting of a revised policy will signify your awareness, acceptance, and compliance with the updated terms.

  3. Information Usage

    1. Service Promotion: We may use your contact information to send you marketing communications, surveys, and to understand your usage patterns to improve our Services. You can unsubscribe from these communications at any time by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in the email or adjusting your notification settings in the app.

    2. Service Provision: We utilize your personal information to deliver the Services you request, including processing transactions, enabling features, synchronizing data across your devices, and providing customer support.

    3. Service Protection: Your information helps us maintain the security and integrity of our Services, including identity verification, fraud prevention, spam and malware detection, and ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.

    4. Legal Compliance: We may use and disclose your information as required by law, including in response to subpoenas, court orders, or regulatory inquiries.

    5. Push Notifications: We may send push notifications to keep you informed about Service updates, new features, or important announcements. You can manage these notifications in your device settings (e.g., enable/disable, adjust frequency) at any time.

    6. Personalized Advertising: We collaborate with third-party advertising networks to present personalized advertisements. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), Norway, or Switzerland, certain networks may require your consent to process your personal data (including device advertising identifier and IP address) for the purpose of delivering relevant ads. This data is retained for up to 6 months and is not shared with third parties for non-advertising purposes. You will be asked to consent to the collection of your device’s advertising identifier and IP address. To opt out of personalized advertising, see the relevant section below.

  4. Information We Collect

    • Personal Information: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, username, password, and other registration details. Additionally, we may collect in-game profile information such as your avatar, player level, and game achievements (this profile information is only linked to your personal identity if you choose to associate it with your account).

    • Device Information: We automatically collect information about the device you use to access the Game, including device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as Android ID or IDFA for iOS), browser type, IP address, and network provider. This information is used to optimize the Service for your device and prevent fraud.

    • Gameplay Information: We collect data related to your gameplay, such as game session duration, in-game actions, progress, and scores. This data is primarily used in anonymized form (data that no longer identifies you individually) to improve the Game and provide a better user experience.

    • Payment Information: If you make in-game purchases, we collect payment details such as credit card information or other payment method data. However, we only use this information for processing payments and do not store it longer than necessary for that purpose (typically 90 days after transaction reconciliation). All payment data is encrypted during transmission and storage.

  5. How We Use Your Information

    • To Provide and Improve the Game: We use your information to operate the Game, personalize your gaming experience, and make improvements and optimizations. For example, we may use your gameplay data (in anonymized form) to balance game difficulty or offer customized in-game content that matches your play style.

    • For Communication: We may use your contact information to send you important notifications about the Game, such as updates, maintenance schedules, or customer support messages. These communications are necessary for the provision of the Service and cannot be unsubscribed from (you may adjust marketing communication preferences separately).

    • Analytics and Research: We analyze aggregated and anonymized data to understand user behavior, preferences, and trends. This helps us develop new features and enhance the overall quality of the Game. Aggregated data (e.g., “80% of users complete Level 5 in 10 minutes”) does not identify any individual user.

  6. Information Retention

    We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Specific retention periods are as follows:

    • Account information (name, email): Retained for 14 months after your last active use (including login, gameplay, or in-app purchase) of the Services; deleted if no activity is recorded within this period.
    • Payment information: Retained only until the transaction is completed and reconciled (typically 90 days), and then anonymized (no longer linked to your account).
    • Gameplay information (non-personalized): Retained for up to 2 years for analytics and service improvement (anonymized after 14 months).
    • Legal compliance data: Retained for the period required by applicable laws (e.g., tax, regulatory reporting).
    After the retention period expires, personal information will be permanently deleted or anonymized. Once deleted, you will no longer have access to the data or the ability to exercise rights such as access, erasure, rectification, or data portability.

  7. Your Rights and Choices

    1. Data Correction and Deletion: You have the right to request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information, or the deletion of your personal information if it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or if you withdraw your consent (where applicable).

    2. Object and Restrict: You can object to or restrict our use or sharing of your data under certain circumstances (e.g., if you believe the data is being used unlawfully, or if you contest the accuracy of the data).

    3. Consent Withdrawal: If you have previously consented to the processing of your data (e.g., for personalized advertising), you may withdraw that consent at any time, such as by disabling camera access in your device settings or opting out of ad tracking. This will not affect data processed prior to the withdrawal.

    4. Opt-Out of Targeted Advertising: To opt out of personalized advertising, you can follow these steps:

      • For iOS Devices: Open Settings, select Privacy & Security, then Advertising and enable “Limit Ad Tracking” (iOS 13 and earlier) or select Tracking and turn off “Allow Apps to Request to Track” (iOS 14+).

      • For Android Devices: Open Settings, select Google > Ads, then enable “Opt out of Ads Personalization” and reset your advertising ID.

      After opting out, you will receive contextual advertisements (based on the content of the app/website) instead of personalized ones (based on your behavior). Note that you may still see non-personalized ads and may receive personalized ads from third-party networks on other apps or websites where you have given consent. For more information, refer to Appendix A below.

      To exercise any of these rights, please contact us atadmin@goldenmindplay.com with the subject line “Privacy Rights Request - [Your Account ID/Email]”. We will verify your identity (to ensure we are disclosing data to the correct person) and process your request free of charge within 45 days (or 90 days if necessary, with written notice to you). If you have unresolved concerns, you may also contact your local data protection authority in the European Economic Area.

      You can also use the Digital Advertising Alliance’s (DAA) tool to opt out of the sale of personal information by some or all of its participating companies. Visithttps://optout.aboutads.info/ (web) or download the DAA’s AppChoices mobile application (iOS/Android).

    California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including:

    • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, or sell about you (we do not sell your personal information to third parties);
    • The right to request deletion of your personal information (subject to certain exceptions, e.g., legal retention requirements);
    • The right to opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell personal information, so this right does not apply);
    • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights (we will not deny you access to the Services or charge you higher fees because you exercised your rights);
    • The right to correct inaccurate personal information;
    • The right to data portability (receive a copy of your personal information in a machine-readable format).
    To exercise these rights, please submit a request toadmin@goldenmindplay.com with the subject line “California Privacy Rights Request - [Your Name/Email]”. We will verify your identity and respond to your request within 45 days (or 90 days if necessary, with notice). For more information about our data practices for California residents, contact us at the email above.

    We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction, including:

If a data breach occurs that affects your personal information (as defined by applicable law, e.g., GDPR, CCPA), we will notify you and relevant regulatory authorities within 72 hours of discovery (or as required by law) and provide steps you can take to protect yourself.
